About

Skimur is a next-gen media sharing, aggregation and personal blogging platform.

Development is currently in progress. A continous delivery system will deliver alpha builds to alpha.skimur.com (not setup yet). During this time, you can see what features are actively being implemented. Before the end of 2015, beta.skimur.com will be released. At that point, we promise to preserve users/posts/settings until Skimur is officailly released (early 2016).
